About GroningenPHP

GroningenPHP is a vibrant PHP user group located in Groningen where PHP developers – both new and experienced – meet, share knowledge, and have fun!

Our History

Our community has been bringing together PHP enthusiasts in the northern Netherlands, hosting regular meetups every first Thursday of the month at various IT companies throughout Groningen. We've grown into a welcoming space where developers can learn about the latest PHP trends, share experiences, and network with like-minded professionals.

What We Do

  • Monthly meetups featuring expert speakers on PHP, Laravel, and related technologies
  • Hands-on workshops and interactive programming sessions
  • Networking opportunities with local tech professionals
  • Knowledge sharing on topics ranging from AI integration to deployment strategies
